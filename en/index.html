Frequently Asked Questions

Are the results on astrolojivakti real?

No. The horoscopes, tarot, numerology, and tests are purely for entertainment and have no scientific basis.

Does the daily horoscope change every day?

Yes, each sign's daily reading changes automatically based on the date and stays the same throughout the day.

How are the compatibility percentages calculated?

They're calculated with a fixed algorithm based on the names/signs you enter — the same pair always gets the same result, it isn't random.

Is my data (name, birth date) saved anywhere?

No, no form data is ever sent to or stored on a server. Every calculation happens in your browser, on your device.

Are the tarot cards really drawn at random?

Yes, all 3 cards are chosen completely at random each time, with a 50% chance of appearing reversed — draw again as many times as you like.

Are there any ads or paid features on the site?

No, astrolojivakti is completely free, no payment is required for any feature.

Can I suggest a new feature?

astrolojivakti is a constantly evolving project — new tests and games keep getting added over time.
